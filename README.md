# 🎱 Free Online Snooker Scoreboard

A free, browser-based **snooker scoreboard and score tracker** — no signup, no app install, no cost. Play solo matches, head-to-head games, or team-of-two doubles, and let the scoreboard handle the maths.

**🔗 Live site:** https://kakhekar.github.io/snooker/

## What it does

This tool is a digital snooker scoreboard for casual players, clubs, and home games. It replaces a chalkboard or notepad with a simple tap-to-score interface for tracking points, breaks, fouls, and frame wins during a snooker match.

## Features

- **Individual or team play** — add any number of players, or set up teams of two
- **One-tap scoring** — tap red, yellow, green, brown, blue, pink, or black to add points instantly
- **Automatic break tracking** — current break and personal best break shown per player
- **Foul handling** — deduct penalty points (4–7) from the offending player in one tap
- **Full match history log** — every point, foul, and turn switch is logged and can be undone
- **Frame tracking** — keep a running tally of frames won across a full match
- **Reds counter** — tracks reds remaining and prompts the correct colour order once reds run out
- **Works on any device** — phone, tablet, or desktop, right in the browser

## Who it's for

- Snooker and pool club players who want a free digital scoreboard
- Anyone tracking casual snooker games at home
- Players who want an easy alternative to a chalkboard or paper scoresheet
- Doubles/team snooker matches needing simple shared scoring

## How to use it

1. Open the [live scoreboard](https://kakhekar.github.io/snooker/)
2. Choose **Individual** or **Teams of two** and enter player names
3. Tap **Rack 'em up** to start
4. Tap balls as they're potted to add points to whoever's break it is
5. Use **Switch turn**, **Foul**, **Undo**, and **End frame** as needed during play

## Tech

A single self-contained HTML file — no build steps, no dependencies, no backend. Runs entirely in the browser and is hosted for free on GitHub Pages.
